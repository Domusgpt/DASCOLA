The Operation

Direct from
Brazilian Waters

D'Ascola Seafood sources whole, fresh fish from the deep waters off Santos, Brazil and ships them overnight via dedicated cold-chain air freight to wholesale distributors, markets, and restaurants across the U.S. East Coast. Every box is packed fresh at origin — never frozen, never warehoused.

Victor D'Ascola maintains direct relationships with Brazilian fishermen and American buyers built over decades. Each shipment arrives with full documentation: species identification, individual weights, box counts, sizing grades, AWB tracking numbers, and transparent pricing — communicated before the fish even lands.

01

Provenance

Every fish we ship has a story — who caught it, where it was landed, and when it left the water. Victor D'Ascola's personal presence at the docks in Santos means nothing ships without his direct inspection and approval. This is how we've operated for generations.

02

Cold Chain

Dedicated air freight corridors from Santos to the East Coast. Fish is packed, iced, and documented at origin, loaded same-day, and arrives at your facility the next morning. Full AWB tracking on every shipment — you always know exactly where your product is.

03

Full Transparency

Before your fish lands, you receive complete documentation: species, box count, individual weights, sizing grades, airway bill numbers, and pricing. No surprises. No ambiguity. You know exactly what's arriving and what it costs — every single time.

04

Daily Reliability

Consistent volume. Consistent quality. Daily shipments to the East Coast's most demanding wholesale markets. When Victor sends a catch forecast, you can build your week around it. That's the kind of reliability that comes from decades of doing this one thing well.

How We Work

A streamlined wholesale operation built on decades of trust. No middlemen, no complexity — just fresh fish from our hands to yours.

01

Door-to-Door Delivery

Every shipment packed, labeled, and documented at origin in Santos. Delivered directly to your facility on the East Coast. No intermediary warehouses, no third-party handlers, no unnecessary touchpoints.

02

Overnight Cold-Chain Air Freight

Fish packed in Santos at dawn arrives at your market the next morning. Dedicated cold-chain corridors with full AWB tracking. Temperature integrity maintained from dock to delivery.

03

Complete Pre-Arrival Documentation

Before your shipment lands, you receive: species, box count, individual weights, sizing grades, pricing, and airway bill numbers. You know exactly what's coming, what it weighs, and what it costs.

04

Volume Forecasting & Planning

Regular catch forecasts based on fleet activity and seasonal patterns. Plan your inventory, commitments, and sales around reliable intelligence — not guesswork.
