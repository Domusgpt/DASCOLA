Fleet Map

Interactive Fishing Fleet Tracker

A canvas-based, animated fleet tracking map for the fishing industry. 7-layer rendering, vib3 water surface effects, weather overlay, expandable vessel/port panels, fleet coordination, and AIS integration. Zero dependencies. Zero build step. Just ES modules.
