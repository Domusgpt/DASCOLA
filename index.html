Our Operation

Direct from Brazil

D'Ascola Seafood is a premier fresh seafood distributor connecting the rich fishing grounds of Brazil directly to the top wholesale markets and restaurants on the U.S. East Coast. Every shipment is air-freighted overnight — packed fresh, never frozen — and delivered to your door with full documentation.

With decades of relationships in both Brazilian fisheries and the American marketplace, we deliver swordfish, tuna, yellowtail, snapper, and specialty catch with the reliability and traceability that serious buyers demand.

01

Tradition

Rooted in generations of Brazilian fishing heritage. Victor D'Ascola's hands-on relationships with fishermen and buyers ensure every shipment meets the highest standard.

02

Logistics

Air-freighted overnight from Santos, Brazil via dedicated cold-chain corridors. Every box arrives with AWB tracking, packing lists, and weight documentation.

03

Integrity

Complete transparency on every shipment. Species, box count, weight, AWB numbers, and pricing — documented and communicated before your fish even lands.

04

Reliability

Daily deliveries to the East Coast's top distributors and markets. Consistent volume, consistent quality. When Victor sends a forecast, you can plan your week around it.

How We Work

A streamlined operation built on decades of trust, designed for the demands of professional wholesale buyers.

Door-to-Door Delivery

Every shipment delivered directly to your facility — packed, labeled, documented. No middlemen, no delays.

Overnight Air Freight

Fish packed in Santos arrives at your East Coast market the next morning via dedicated cold-chain air freight with full AWB tracking.

Packing Lists & Documentation

Species, box count, individual weights, sizing grades, pricing, and airway bill numbers — sent before the fish lands.

Volume Forecasting

Regular forecasts on upcoming catch so you can plan your inventory. We communicate what's coming and when.
